General Info

The photo above is of a place in Boracay, a tourist attraction in the Philippines.

The Philippines is a Southeast Asian country that consists of 7,641 islands divided into the regions of Luzon, Visayas, and Mindanao

Manila is its capital, while Quezon City is its largest city.

It has a population of about 118 million, consisting of an Austronesian-based cultural heritage with a lot of Spanish, American, and Chinese influences.

Filipino and English are the official languages, though more than 170 dialects and languages are spoken across the country.

The country is a republic, wherein a president serves as chief of state, and most of the people are Roman Catholic.

Its economy depends on service industries such as BPO, remittances from overseas Filipino workers, agriculture, tourism, and electronics.

The Philippines is renowned for its beautiful beaches, volcanoes, and biodiversity, while also being prone to typhoons and earthquakes, located in the vicinity of the Pacific Ring of Fire.
